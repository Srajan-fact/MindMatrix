5 Dos and Don'ts when reviewing others' work:

Dos: Be specific about what needs to change and why. Focus on the work, not the person. Acknowledge what's working before pointing out issues. Ask questions when something is unclear. Keep the end goal in mind — make it better, not just different.
Don'ts: Don't rewrite everything just because you'd do it differently. Don't use vague feedback like "this doesn't work." Don't nitpick minor preferences while missing bigger issues. Don't make it personal. Don't rush through a review just to tick a box.
