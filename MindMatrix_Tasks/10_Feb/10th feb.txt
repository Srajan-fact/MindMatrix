What is the score / result of your Communication Test?

I took the Professional Communication Test and the experience was genuinely eye-opening. It assessed how well I structure my thoughts, listen actively, and convey information clearly in a professional setting. The test pushed me to think about not just what I say, but how I say it — tone, clarity, and conciseness all came into play. It reinforced that communication isn't just about speaking well; it's about making sure the other person actually understands you.

Share briefly your experience of undergoing SWOT analysis

Doing a SWOT analysis on myself was uncomfortable at first — it's not easy to sit down and honestly list out your weaknesses. But that discomfort is kind of the point. The strengths section felt good, but the real value came from mapping out weaknesses and threats, because those are the things you actually need a plan for. The opportunities section shifted my thinking from passive to proactive — I started seeing resources around me that I'd been ignoring. Overall it felt less like a school exercise and more like a honest conversation with myself about where I'm headed.
